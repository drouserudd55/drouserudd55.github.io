About

Hi, wanna know more about me? You've come to the right place! My name is Daniel Rouse-Rudd. I enjoy programming, video games, hiking, and playing guitar. I started coding in 2014 while I was at Columbus State Community College and absolutley loved it. I obtained my associates in software development in 2018.



During my time learning programming, I had a pretty rough start, and honestly hated it for a while. However, through dedication I stuck with it, and ended up loving it! The pure satisfaction gained from creating something brought pure joy. There is no better feeling than working for hours on something, only to finally find the flaw, and get it up and running. This passion is what drives me to get better. This passion also led me to studying the world of IT as well! I have been working in various IT jobs since I graduated, and have admittidley gotten rusty during this time. However I am currently working through some Udemy courses to clean the rust off these old gears. Hopefully sometiume throughout the next few months this site should improve significantly
