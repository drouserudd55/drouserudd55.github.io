About

Hi, wanna know more about me? You've come to the right place! My name is Daniel Rouse-Rudd. I enjoy programming, video games, and long walks on the beach. I started coding about three years ago after I learned that I hated marketing while I was at Columbus State Community College. After I learned my hatred for marketing, I began searching for where my true passions lies, and that was within a computer.



During my time learning programming, I had a pretty rough start, and honestly hated it for a while. However, through dedication I stuck with it, and ended up loving it! The pure satisfaction gained from creating something brought pure joy. There is no better feeling than working for hours on something, only to finally find the flaw, and get it up and running. This passion is what drives me to get better.
